## FAQs

**Q: What does TG20:21 mean?**
A: It’s the NASC technical guide for safe access—our scaffolds are designed to meet its latest specs.

**Q: How quickly can you erect scaffolding?**
A: For smaller residential projects, usually within 24–48 hours. Larger or bespoke scaffolds may require [Design & Drawings](/services/scaffolding-design-drawings).

**Q: Do you provide handover certificates?**
A: Yes. Every scaffold is inspected and certified on completion and at regular intervals through our [Inspection & Maintenance](/services/scaffolding-inspections-maintenance) service.
