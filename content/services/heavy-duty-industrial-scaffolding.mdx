## FAQs

**Q: What does TG20:21 mean?**  
A: It’s the NASC technical guide for safe access—our scaffolds are designed to meet its latest specs.

**Q: How quickly can you erect scaffolding?**  
A: For smaller residential projects, usually within 24–48 hours. Larger or bespoke scaffolds may require design and scheduling.

**Q: Do you provide handover certificates?**  
A: Yes. Every scaffold is inspected and certified on completion and at regular intervals.
