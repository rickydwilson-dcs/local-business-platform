# Areas We Cover

Colossus Scaffolding provides safe, compliant scaffolding services throughout the **South East UK**.
From small residential works to complex commercial projects, we’ve got you covered.

---

## Key Areas
- East Sussex (Hastings, St Leonards on Sea, Bexhill, Eastbourne, Rye, Lewes, Uckfield, Hailsham, Newhaven, Seaford)
- West Sussex
- Kent
- Surrey

## FAQs

**Q: Do you work outside the South East?**
A: Our core coverage is the South East, but larger projects outside the region can be considered.

**Q: Which towns do you serve in East Sussex?**
A: Hastings, St Leonards on Sea, Bexhill, Eastbourne, Rye, Lewes, Uckfield, Hailsham, Newhaven and Seaford.
