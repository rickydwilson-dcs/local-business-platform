# Health & Safety

We take health & safety seriously. Our scaffolds are designed, erected and inspected to TG20:21 standards.

---

## FAQs

**Q: How often are scaffolds inspected?**
A: Every 7 days and after adverse weather or alterations.

**Q: Do you provide handover certificates?**
A: Yes, on every scaffold erected.
