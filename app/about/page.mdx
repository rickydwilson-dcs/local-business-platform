# About Us

At **Colossus Scaffolding**, safety and professionalism come first.
We provide TG20:21-compliant scaffolding solutions for residential, commercial and industrial projects across the South East UK.

With CHAS and Constructionline accreditations, CISRS-qualified teams and £10m public liability insurance, we deliver peace of mind on every job.

---

## Our Values
- **Safety First**: TG20:21 compliant scaffolds, inspected regularly.
- **Local Service**: Responsive teams covering towns across the South East.
- **Trusted Partner**: Accreditations, insurance and proven experience.

---

## FAQs

**Q: Are your scaffolders qualified?**
A: Yes. All scaffolders are CISRS or CITB trained.

**Q: Do you provide insurance?**
A: Every project is backed by £10m public liability insurance.

**Q: Where do you operate?**
A: We cover all of the South East of England, including East Sussex, West Sussex, Kent, Surrey and London.
